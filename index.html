MOTIVATION..

There is a common saying that photographs speak a million words. Photographs have the power to evoke nostalgia. They serve as a chronicle of our lives, where we have been, what we have done, who we were. They are the way to tell story that we don’t always speak. They are the legacy we leave behind our children, offering a way to share our stories with generations to come.

WHAT I DO?

Photography is an amazing art that allows us not to just capture moments, but, to freeze all emotions associated with those moments in time. It allows you to capture the beauty of amazing corners of the earth.
Many people opt for photography these days as it is powerful enough to start movements and invoke change in one’s life. A synonym of communication, photography is used to express ideas, to illustrate, to report, to document and to form as time travel.
As photography has undergone many transformations and the process is still going on, technology has expanded its visual possibilities, with different apparatuses and lenses making it possible for us to capture different types of photography in ways we wouldn’t have dreamed before. 

CRUX !

A photograph can be expressed through countless ways through various photography techniques like aerial, black and white, composite, infrared, dance, food, macro, interior, documentary and natural landscape photography. Once a formal and scheduled affair showcasing posed, somber faces photography has now given way to spontaneous, impulsive, informal photo shoots.
Photography adds as an emotional factor which plays a major role in branding of a product. In terms of business purpose it is massive profit.

"SOMETHING ABOUT ME"

Photography is my calling, my profession, and the thing that will undoubtedly drive me insane someday. I don’t photograph subjects. I photograph the way they make me feel. Admittedly, it’s a bit of a strange concept. But it’s honest – and it’s the best way to describe my approach to the craft. 

I wrestle with every image I shoot.

I assume perfection is possible and I bring it out of every picture.If that's all you ever know about me,it's enough to say you know me well,very well.
